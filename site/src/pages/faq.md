## Where does the video come from?

The chapter pages stream the public Viebit MP4 for each NYC Council meeting directly in a video.js player.

## Are transcripts official?

No. Benchmark transcripts are generated from public captions and may contain errors. Official transcripts can arrive later through Legistar and may be used for future correction passes.

## Why do some speakers say “Speaker”?

The benchmark data does not include the future diarized, named-speaker ASR output. The site preserves the schema and timestamp behavior now, then can display names when the pipeline produces them.

## Transcription errors

Automated captions and ASR can mishear names, legislation numbers, and fast exchanges. Use the video timestamp as the source of truth when precision matters.
